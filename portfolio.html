Portfolio

Dennis has worked as a copywriter, blogger, SEO specialist and web producer for some, if not all of these clients for the Asian American market.

Breaking it down, he has produced ad copy for the Chinese market for Citibank and served as consultant for the translation of an America the Beautiful into Filipino for Coke. He has aligned client's objectives in targeting DISH for the Chinese, Filipino and Japanese markets. For eight years, he also provided editorial and creative direction for Western Union as it set its sights mainly on capturing the Filipino American market.
